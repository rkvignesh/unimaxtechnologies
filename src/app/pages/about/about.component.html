Who We Are

Established in 2009, Unimax Technologies has marked an enviable presence in the engineering industry. We are a partnership-owned organization committed to delivering excellence in mechanical, civil, and automobile engineering.

Our mission is to provide high-quality engineering solutions by consistently exceeding customer expectations with cost-effective, timely assistance and competent design, drafting, and analysis services.

Combined with cutting-edge production facilities and years of industrial experience, we have become a trusted partner for businesses seeking innovation and reliability.
